University - Placement & Career Development Cell

Dear Students,

The placement season for the academic year has started.

Students interested in campus placements can contact 
placements@au.edu.in.

For internship opportunities, please write to
 internships@au.edu.in.
 
If you have any questions regarding aptitude training sessions,
 email training@au.edu.in.

For resume review and career guidance, 
contact careersupport@au.edu.in.

Companies interested in recruitment drives may reach us at
 recruiters@au.edu.in.

Technical support for the placement portal is available at 
portalhelp@au.edu.in.

For scholarship-related queries, contact
 scholarships@au.edu.in.

Students participating in hackathons can send their registrations to
 hackathons@au.edu.in.

For workshop and seminar information, write to 
events@au.edu.in.

Any concerns regarding student records should be sent to 
records@au.edu.in.

For international student support, contact
 international.office@au.edu.in.

Thank you.
